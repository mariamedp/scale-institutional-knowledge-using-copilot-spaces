# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

This README serves as the entry point for OctoAcme's project management process documentation. It provides brief context and direct links to all process documents used in our programs.

## Project Management Process Summary

OctoAcme's project management process is structured to promote clarity, stakeholder alignment, and iterative delivery across cross-functional teams. At the heart of OctoAcme's approach are well-defined project lifecycles that begin with rigorous initiation—validating business needs, aligning stakeholders, and defining clear success metrics—followed by actionable planning that breaks work into shippable increments and identifies risks and dependencies. Project execution leverages disciplined rhythms such as daily standups, weekly syncs, and sprint reviews, all supported by structured documentation like one-pagers, prioritized backlogs, risk registers, and release plans. Each project is guided through initiation, planning, execution, release, and continuous improvement phases to ensure that goals remain visible and progress is tracked.

The key personas in OctoAcme's methodology—Project Managers, Product Managers, Developers, and QA/Testing—each play defined roles to safeguard project success. Project Managers orchestrate delivery activities, manage risk, and facilitate communication; Product Managers define outcomes, prioritize work, and validate solutions; Developers build features, participate in planning, and uphold standards for code quality; and QA professionals ensure that acceptance criteria and testing requirements are met. Clear role separation fosters accountability and efficient collaboration, with regular updates and decision logs facilitating transparency.

Effective communication underpins every aspect of the process. Weekly PM and Product Manager syncs, daily standups for the delivery team, stakeholder briefings, and milestone-based updates ensure that status, risks, and decisions are systematically shared. Key templates—status reports, incident communications, and escalation pathways—standardize information flow across teams and stakeholders. For risk management, the process includes regular identification and review, clear mitigation planning, and structured escalation paths to address blockers at the appropriate level.

Quality assurance and continuous improvement are deeply embedded. OctoAcme enforces robust testing—unit, integration, and end-to-end—plus security scanning and manual QA before feature acceptance. Releases follow documented checklists for pre-release criteria, deployment, and rollback plans. Retrospectives after milestones or incidents close the loop, with learnings converted into actionable backlog items and iteratively measured for impact. This disciplined, role-driven approach accelerates onboarding, drives consistency, and provides a living, versioned knowledge base for all contributors.
